PhD Student in Astrophysics · University of Notre Dame

I study neutral hydrogen (H I) in the circumgalactic medium (CGM) of nearby galaxies using radio observations and optical spectroscopy.

Research

My work focuses on the structure and kinematics of low-column density H I in galaxy halos and the physical processes that shape it.

  • Single-dish + interferometric comparisons (GBT / WSRT)
  • Beam effects, contamination modeling, and systematics
  • Optical spectroscopy follow-up and multiwavelength context
